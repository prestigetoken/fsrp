CAD/MDT System

We offer a CAD/MDT System to better enhance your Roleplaying experience! It's a unique system that you wont find in any other community. *To get access to our CAD/MDT, you must be a member!*

We have a Team Speak 3 server, which gives a more realistic feel when patrolling, and its for all Departments! *To get access to our Team Speak 3 server, you must be a member!*
